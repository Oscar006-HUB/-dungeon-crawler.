📖 The Story

You are a lone warrior who has descended into CRYPTDEPTH — an ancient, cursed dungeon that shifts and rebuilds itself with every visit. Monsters lurk in the shadows, treasure glitters in forgotten rooms, and somewhere below lies the final floor. Nobody has ever returned. Will you?

⚔️ How To Play

Move through procedurally generated rooms. When you walk into an enemy you automatically attack them. Enemies fight back on their turn. Find loot, level up, and descend deeper using the glowing stairs. Each floor gets harder — enemies are stronger, more numerous, but also worth more XP.

🎒 Items
⚡
Charge Pack (Potion) — Restores 8–15 HP. Walk into it to pick up.
⚔️
Blade (Weapon) — Permanently increases your ATK stat.
🛡️
Plate (Armor) — Permanently increases your DEF stat, reducing damage taken.
🪜
Stairs — Descend to the next floor. Located in the furthest room.
⬆️ Leveling Up

Kill enemies to earn XP. When your XP bar fills you level up — gaining +8 Max HP and +2 ATK. Enemies get tougher each floor, but so do you. Strategy matters: don't rush into rooms full of enemies without healing first.

🖥️ Keyboard (Computer)
W A S D Move Up / Left / Down / Right
↑ ← ↓ → Arrow Keys also move the warrior
📱 Mobile (Touch)
D-PAD Use the on-screen directional buttons below the map
HOLD Hold a D-pad button to keep moving in that direction
⚔️ Combat
BUMP Walk into an enemy to attack. No separate attack button needed!
WALK Walk into items & stairs to automatically pick up / descend
💡 Tips

🔦 Only visible tiles are shown — explore carefully.
💀 Enemies only move when YOU move — think before stepping.
🏃 Retreat to hallways to fight enemies one at a time.
⚡ Grab potions early — you'll need them on deep floors.
🗺️ Stairs are always in the last room generated — explore fully!
